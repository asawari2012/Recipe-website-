Delicious Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring gently.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings!
